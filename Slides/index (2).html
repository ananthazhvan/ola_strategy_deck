Strategic Asset
06 / 11
Asset or Stranded Risk?

The Gigafactory — Both

The Build — Impressive Execution

0 → 6 GWh in 24 Months

  • Cells: ~2,000 pilot → ~100,000 cumulative — 50× scale-up
  • Battery cost: $130 → $85/kWh — 35% reduction
  • BOM reduction: 0% → 28% achieved, 40% target by Mar 2028
  • Only manufacturer qualifying for both PLI schemes (Auto + Cell)
  • Awarded 20 GWh under Cell PLI — highest allocation to any recipient
The Risk — Underutilization

16% Utilization

  • 1M unit capacity → sold 164K — massive excess capacity
  • In-house $85/kWh vs global LFP $80/kWh — still 6% above import parity
  • Economically justified only at Phase 2: 12 GWh, $75/kWh (Jul 2027)
  • Global cell overcapacity: ~900 GWh excess — Chinese dumping suppresses prices
  • Cell PLI milestone risk: failure = 2× shortfall deducted from subsidy

Marketing Campaign Plan
07 / 11
₹80 Cr · Radical Transparency Campaign

Engineers, Not Influencers

Ola's brand trust is destroyed. No ad campaign fixes that. Only radical, verifiable transparency rebuilds desire. Four pillars, one message: "We have nothing to hide anymore."

I.
Live Factory
24/7 Gigafactory live stream. Public battery test data. Real-time production counter. Let the factory be the ad — no studio, no filters.
35% budget · YouTube + Owned platforms
II.
Service Dashboard
Public, real-time service metrics: parts availability %, turnaround time, NPS score, recall resolution rate. Updated weekly. Anyone can check.
25% budget · Digital OOH + PR
III.
Engineers Not Influencers
Ola's chief engineer hosts monthly deep-dive videos: battery chemistry, QA process, Gen 3 platform walkthrough. Technical credibility as brand building.
25% budget · YouTube + Podcasts
IV.
The Recall Promise
48-hour recall guarantee with loaner scooter + ₹5,000 compensation. Advertised boldly — the guarantee itself is the campaign hook.
15% budget · Social + Referral program

Success metrics: NPS from negative to +20 in 12 months. App rating 3.2 → 4.0. Service complaints down 60%. Brand sentiment shift from "unreliable" to "transparent and improving."
