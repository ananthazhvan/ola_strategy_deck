## Overview

[[Companies/Ola Electric]] is a pure EV player in India building vertically integrated technology and manufacturing capabilities. Founded by [[Companies/Bhavish Aggarwal]], it operates India's largest company-owned EV experience centre network (935 centres) and is building an integrated EV hub in Tamil Nadu.
