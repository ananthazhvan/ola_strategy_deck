# Interest Cost

> [!data] Source: ola/PROFIT_AND_LOSS.csv
> Ola's interest cost grew from Rs 0.79 Cr (Mar-21) to Rs 366 Cr (Mar-25) — a 463x increase — driven by Gigafactory debt financing. Interest now exceeds total revenue of FY21 (Rs 0.86 Cr) by 425x. The debt service burden is a structural cost that cannot be cut.
> Causal chain: Rising debt to fund CapEx and operating losses created growing interest burden that compounded losses.
