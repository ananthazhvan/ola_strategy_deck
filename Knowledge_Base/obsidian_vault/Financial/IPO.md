# Initial Public Offering (IPO)

> [!insight] The Single Most Important Thing
> Ola's IPO in August 2024 served as a critical inflection point, infusing the company with ₹5,429 Cr but simultaneously forcing them into the public eye right as the [[Service Crisis]] began to peak.

> [!data] Source: CASH_FLOW_AND_DERIVED.csv
> The IPO generated ₹5,429 Cr in net proceeds, which funded the cumulative ₹4,000 Cr capex required to build the [[Technology/Gigafactory_Updates|Gigafactory]] over FY24-FY25.

> [!data] Source: SERVICE_CRISIS_TIMELINE.csv
> At the time of the IPO in August 2024, the DRHP disclosed 189 court cases related to service and quality. However, the "IPO hype" minimized the market's initial reaction. By October 2024, the reality of the service issues caught up with the stock as the [[CCPA]] intervened, causing the stock price to plunge along with the [[Market Share Collapse]].

> [!data] Source: risk_factors.txt
> The IPO documentation exhaustively listed risks such as "battery fires," "service infrastructure scaling," and "phantom revenue recognition," all of which manifested violently in late 2024 and 2025.
