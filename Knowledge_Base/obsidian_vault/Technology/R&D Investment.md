> Ola spent ₹507.7 Cr on R&D in FY23 (18.3% of revenue) across India, UK, and US — capitalising 83% as intangible assets — but with only 164K units sold in FY26, the amortisation burden per vehicle is structurally high for a [[Strategy/Operating Leverage|low-volume manufacturer]].

## Capitalized Development

Significant R&D is capitalized as "intangible assets under development":
- FY22: ₹160.2 Cr additions
- FY23: ₹421.6 Cr additions
- Q1 FY24: ₹82.7 Cr additions

This means a large portion of R&D spend doesn't hit the P&L immediately but creates future amortization burden.
