# Generation 2 Platform

**Category:** Core Technology
**Status:** In Production (since July 2023)

---

## Overview

The Generation 2 platform is the common EV architecture underpinning all current Ola S1 scooter models. It represents a significant improvement over the Generation 1 platform.

---

## Key Specifications (Ola S1 Pro Comparison)

| Feature | Gen 1 | Gen 2 |
|---------|-------|-------|
| Acceleration | Baseline | Enhanced |
| Top Speed | Baseline | Improved |
| Range | Baseline | Extended |
| Motor Power | Baseline | Higher |
| Battery Performance | Baseline | Better |

---

## Component Commonality

**68.85% of components are common** across three scooter models built on Gen 2:
- Ola S1 Pro
- Ola S1 Air
- Ola S1 X+

### Benefits of Platform Approach:
1. **Faster product development** — New models built from common architecture
2. **Lower BOM cost** — Shared components increase purchasing scale
3. **Reduced R&D spend per model** — Amortize development across multiple products
4. **Manufacturing efficiency** — Common assembly processes, tooling
5. **Faster time to market** — 5 new scooter models announced after first product

---

## In-House Designed Components
- Electric powertrain (modular design)
- Battery pack with BMS (varies in size by model)
- Motors
- Power electronics modules
- Vehicle frames (manufacturing started Aug 2023)
