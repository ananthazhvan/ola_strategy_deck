# Platform Evolution (Gen 3+)

**Potential Impact:** 🟡 MEDIUM
**Timeline:** Medium-term

---

## The Thesis

If [[Technology/Gen 2 Platform|Gen 2]] delivered 68.85% component commonality and margin improvement from -5.4% → 13.2%, future platform generations (Gen 3, Gen 4) can continue driving cost reduction and performance improvement.

---

## What Gen 3+ Could Enable

1. **Higher component commonality** — Target 80%+ across all models (scooters + motorcycles)
2. **Simplified manufacturing** — Fewer unique parts = faster assembly, lower defect rates
3. **Next-gen battery integration** — Structural battery packs, cell-to-pack technology
4. **Software-defined vehicle architecture** — Centralized compute, OTA-upgradable hardware
5. **Shared motorcycle + scooter platform** — One platform for all 2W form factors

---

## Prerequisites

1. **Sustained R&D investment** — [[Technology/R&D Investment|₹1,600 Cr IPO allocation]] committed
2. **Gen 2 proven at scale** — Must demonstrate reliability before next evolution
3. **Gigafactory online** — In-house cells enable platform-level battery optimization
4. **Financial runway** — Platform development takes years and significant capital
