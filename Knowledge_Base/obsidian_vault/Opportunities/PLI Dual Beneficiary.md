# PLI Dual Beneficiary Advantage

**Potential Impact:** 🔴 HIGH
**Timeline:** Immediate (5-year window)

---

## The Unique Position

Ola Electric is the **ONLY EV manufacturer in India** that is a beneficiary of **TWO Government of India PLI schemes** simultaneously:

1. **Automobile PLI Scheme** — 13-18% of determined sales value
2. **Cell PLI Scheme** — 20 GWh capacity (largest allocation to any recipient)

---

## Financial Value

### Automobile PLI
- 13-18% of DSV for 5 consecutive years starting FY2023
- Applies to every eligible AAT vehicle sold
- Requires 50% domestic value addition (enabled by vertical integration)

### Cell PLI
- Per-kWh cash incentive based on value addition % and actual cell sales
- Quarterly disbursement over 5 years from Gigafactory commissioning
- 20 GWh total committed capacity

---

## Competitive Barrier

| Competitor | Auto PLI | Cell PLI |
|------------|----------|----------|
| [[Companies/Ola Electric]] | ✅ 13-18% DSV | ✅ 20 GWh |
| [[TVS Motor]] | May qualify | ❌ |
| [[Bajaj Auto]] | May qualify | ❌ |
| [[Companies/Hero MotoCorp]] | May qualify | ❌ |
| [[Ather Energy]] | May qualify | ❌ |

Even if competitors qualify for Auto PLI, **none have Cell PLI benefits**. This gives Ola a 5-year window of structural cost advantage if cell manufacturing succeeds.

---

## Time Sensitivity

- Auto PLI: FY2023-FY2027 (5 years)
- Cell PLI: 5 years from Gigafactory commissioning
- First-mover advantage erodes if competitors also qualify for future PLI rounds
