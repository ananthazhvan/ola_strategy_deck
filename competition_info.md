### Problem Statement (as provided)

> India remains one of the most attractive yet challenging markets for ambitious brands seeking long-term growth. With its expanding digital economy, rising consumer aspirations, diverse demographics, and rapidly evolving preferences, the country offers unmatched opportunities for companies looking to scale. Yet, success in India requires far more than strong funding or global recognition. Companies must navigate intense competition, price sensitivity, shifting consumer behavior, and the need for constant localization.
>
> Several startups and modern brands have built remarkable success stories—either globally or within India itself. However, sustaining that momentum has proven difficult. Strategic missteps, operational gaps, changing market realities, and stronger competition have caused many once-prominent players to lose relevance or face serious challenges.
>
> In such an environment, rebranding, sharper execution, and renewed market strategy often determine whether a company can lead again.
>
> This year, participants must choose one company and develop a compelling revival, repositioning, or growth strategy that can help it succeed in its next phase.
>
> **Company to work on:** Ola Electric
>
> Ola Electric entered the market with bold ambition—to accelerate India’s electric mobility transition and build a globally competitive EV brand. Backed by strong visibility, rapid expansion, and a futuristic vision, the company quickly became one of the most talked-about names in the sector. However, product quality concerns, after-sales service gaps, and rising competition created friction between brand promise and customer experience.
>
> At the same time, India’s EV market is only beginning its long-term growth journey. Consumers are increasingly open to sustainable mobility, while policy support and charging infrastructure continue to evolve. Ola Electric still holds strong recall and first-mover momentum.
>
> **Your task is to reposition Ola Electric as India’s most reliable, aspirational, and scalable EV ecosystem by improving trust, service excellence, innovation, and long-term profitability.**
